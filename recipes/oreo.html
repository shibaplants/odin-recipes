Oreo Cookie

Ingredients

  • All Purpose Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Chopped Oreos
  • Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Large Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  1. Preheat the oven to 350ยบ F.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, salt, Oreos, and chocolate chips. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar with an electric mixer
    until blended, about 1 minute. Add the eggs and vanilla, beat again until mixed
  4. Gradually add in the flour mixture, mixing on low or with a spatual just until combined.
  5. Use a cookie scoop to form dough balls. Press 6-8 cookies on a tray, about 3 inches apart.
    Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are light brown and the center is puffy.
  6. Allow cookies to cool for 15 minutes before removing them from the sheet. Store in an air tight container.
